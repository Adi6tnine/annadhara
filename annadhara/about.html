About Annadhara

At Annadhara, we are dedicated to reducing food waste and fighting hunger. Our mission is to connect surplus food donors with recipients in need, ensuring that no food goes to waste.

Our Mission

We strive to create a sustainable food system where surplus food is redirected to those who need it most. We believe that everyone deserves access to nutritious food.

Our Vision

We envision a world where food waste is minimized and hunger is eradicated. Through community engagement and innovation, we aim to foster a culture of sharing and sustainability.

Why We Started

Our journey began with the realization that hunger affects millions worldwide. Inspired by the stories of those in need, we set out to create a platform to combat food waste and hunger.
